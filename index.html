Calculation Notes

1. Flow formula (pitot method): Qc = 29.83 × Cd × D² × P½

Cd = discharge coefficient  |  D = orifice diameter (IN)  |  P = pitot pressure (PSI)

2. Flow projection formula: Qp = Qc × [(Ps−Px) / (Ps−Pr)]0.54

Ps = static pressure  |  Pr = residual (test)  |  Px = target residual
